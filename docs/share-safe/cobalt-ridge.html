Overview

Cobalt Ridge Technologies (EXAMPLE — fictional company) is an SDVOSB range operations specialist concentrated at Edwards-area test activities. [S1] They sub for larger primes on consolidated contracts and prime small-business set-aside range task orders. Growing fast; hired two former government range officers into BD roles in 2025. [S2]

Threat Summary

Dangerous on small-business set-asides at AFTC and as a low-cost sub that strengthens a big prime's price story. Not yet a credible prime above ~$25M, but trending that way.

Their Discriminators

  • Genuine range-operations depth: their staff ran these ranges in uniform. [S2]
  • SDVOSB status opens set-aside paths we cannot follow. [S1]

Strengths

  • Low cost structure; consistently undercuts on labor rates. [S3]
  • Customer-favorite technical staff on the current range O&M subcontract. [S2]

Weaknesses

W1 — No corporate infrastructure for large prime work

They have never primed above $25M and lack the program controls, purchasing system, and DCAA-approved accounting story a large consolidated contract demands. [S1]

W2 — Key-person dependence

Their range credibility concentrates in two former range officers; both are past federal retirement age and BD-focused rather than execution-focused. [S2]
