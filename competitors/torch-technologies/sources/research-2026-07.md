## Company basics

- Founded October 2002, Huntsville AL, by Bill Roark and Don Holder.
  100% employee-owned; ESOP established 2004 (one source says steps began
  2005), 100% employee-owned by ~2010-2011 (sources differ; company NCEO
  release says "by the end of 2011"; profile page says S-corp ESOP 2011).
  Parent: Starfish Holdings, Inc. (ESOP holding company; also owns Freedom
  Real Estate & Capital and SIMVANA). Became a public benefit corporation
  in 2023.
  - https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Torch_Technologies (accessed 2026-07-17)
  - http://www.torchtechnologies.com/company/company-profile/ (accessed 2026-07-17)

  - https://starfishholdings.com/starfish-holdings-announces-changes-to-management-team/ (accessed 2026-07-17)
- Primary customers per own profile: Army AMCOM and MDA; also Navy and Air
  Force. Retention claimed "well over 90%". Employee-owners in CO, FL, HI,
  MD, MA, MI, NM, OH, OK, TX, VA, DC, Kwajalein, Egypt. Offices: Huntsville
  (HQ), Aberdeen MD, Albuquerque NM, Boston MA, Colorado Springs CO,
