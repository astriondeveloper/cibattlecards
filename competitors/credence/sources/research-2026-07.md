# Research notes — Credence Management Solutions (2026-07)

Initial card research, all public sources. Collected 2026-07-17 for the
`credence` battle card. Method note: this environment's outbound proxy
returned HTTP 403 on direct page fetches (including credence-llc.com,
credence.ai, washingtontechnology.com, gao.gov, usaspending.gov/api), so
every source below was collected from web search results and search-engine
snippets/summaries of the listed URLs on the access date. Facts were kept
only where the URL and the snippet content matched. No paywalled, internal,
or improperly obtained material was used. Nothing resembling CUI or
source-selection data was encountered.

  PitchBook; a 2011 WashingtonExec interview describes the founding circa
  2007 — treat exact year as reported-range). HQ: 8609 Westwood Center Dr,
  Suite 300, Vienna, VA. Founder & CEO: Siddhartha (Sid) Chowdhary.
  President: Prashant Gaur, joined 2011-10-21 (ex-PwC partner, ex-DoD
  Director of Enterprise Integration, past ACT-IAC president); some profiles

## USAID / global health franchise and its collapse

  USAID task order (7200AA21N00004), won 2022; Team Credence with Public
  Health Institute; survived Tetra Tech GAO protests (incl. B-420305.7,
  denied — challenge to continued performance during corrective action;
