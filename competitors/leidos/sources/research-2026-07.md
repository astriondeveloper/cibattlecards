## Company baseline

- HQ: Reston, VA. Among the largest US federal technology/services primes.
- FY2025 (fiscal year ended 2026-01-02): revenue ~$17.2B; net bookings ~$17.5B;
  adjusted EBITDA margin ~14%; ~48,000 employees (some sources cite 45,000-47,000).
- 87% of FY2025 revenue from the US government (FY2025 10-K).
- FY2025 reportable segments: National Security & Digital; Health & Civil;
  Commercial & International; Defense Systems.
- FY2026 realignment (effective first day of FY2026, prior years recast):
  Intelligence & Digital; Health; Homeland; Defense.
- Q1 FY2026: revenue $4.4B; total backlog ~$48.369B (funded $9.598B);
  FY2026 guidance raised to $18.0B-$18.4B; Defense ~$883M (integrated air defense
  up, airborne surveillance winding down).
- CEO: Thomas (Tom) Bell (since 2023). Strategy: NorthStar 2030, five growth
  pillars (space and maritime; energy infrastructure; digital modernization and
  cyber; highly customized critical mission software; managed health services).
- Other leaders: Chris Cage (EVP & CFO, at company since 1996); Cindy Gruensfelder
  (President, Defense Systems Sector); Steve Hull (President, Digital Modernization
  Sector); Liz Porter (President, Health & Civil Sector); Jim Carlini (CTO).
- History: 2013 SAIC separation (OCI/SETA-driven); 2016 Lockheed Martin IS&GS
  (>$5B, ~doubled the company); 2020 Dynetics ($1.65B); 2021 Gibbs & Cox (~$375-380M);
  2025 Kudu Dynamics (~$293M/$300M); 2026 ENTRUST Solutions Group (~$2.4B).

## Collision surface with Astrion

- Redstone/Huntsville: Dynetics (>3,000 staff) builds hypersonic glide bodies,
  IFPC air-defense launchers, missiles, plus GETS test-station equipment - directly
  inside our Army aviation-and-missile customer base (DEVCOM/AvMC, AMCOM, PEO M&S).
- Air Force sustainment/test: F-16 avionics product-support integration ($475M,
  automatic test systems / TPS), Cloud One ($455M), F-16 FMS IDIQ (up to $987M).
- Digital modernization: DES/DoDNet ($11.5B DISA), CESO ($142M), CDES ($35M).
- Missile defense: Golden Dome niche ($4-5B aligned; radar, sensing, c-UAS, air def).
- Consolidated recompetes: default giant with RS3, SeaPort-NxG, Alliant 2, OASIS+.
