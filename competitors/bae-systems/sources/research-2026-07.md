## Key findings for the card

- BAE Systems, Inc. = US arm of BAE Systems plc (UK). HQ Falls Church, VA.
  ~35,000-36,200 US employees (Dec 2024). Company bio: US sales approaching
  $13.6B; top-10 Pentagon supplier. President & CEO Tom Arseneault since
  Apr 1, 2020 (2026 Wash100).
- US structure: Electronic Systems (EW, EO/IR, flight/engine controls, precision
  guidance/seekers, comms/data links, power & propulsion), Platforms & Services
  US (combat vehicles, munitions, naval guns, ship repair, GOCO plants),
  Intelligence & Security (systems engineering/integration/sustainment services;
  GXP geospatial; SIGINT; cross-domain/cyber; ICBM sustainment; Azalea sats),
  and Space & Mission Systems (former Ball Aerospace).
- Ball Aerospace acquired Feb 16, 2024 for $5.6B; renamed Space & Mission
  Systems; ~5,200 US employees, 60%+ cleared; Broomfield CO.
- Parent plc FY2024: sales £28.3B (+~14% cc); US ~44% of sales; underlying EBIT
  ~£3.0B; record order backlog £77.8B.
- Foreign-ownership governance: BAE Systems, Inc. operates under a Special
  Security Agreement (US Gov / BAE Systems, Inc. / BAE Systems plc) with
  US-citizen outside directors overseeing security and export compliance.

### Services collision points (the actual reason they're on our list)

- ICBM ISC 2.0 (RFP FA8207-21-R-0001): AF systems engineering & integration for
  Minuteman III sustainment + Sentinel; 5-yr base, up to 18-yr PoP; award
  reported $3.3B-$3.8B, program value cited up to ~$12B. BAE won 2022 →
  Guidehouse protest sustained → re-eval → Guidehouse won Feb 2024 → BAE protest
  sustained (GAO B-420860.4/.5/.6) → AF cancelled Aug 2024, restarted.
- $699M Army DoD Supercomputing Resource Center (DSRC) support (2022, to Jun 15
  2027): operates AFRL/ARL/ERDC/Navy DSRCs; enables RDT&E + acquisition eng.
- Recent sustaining-engineering IDIQs: Navy airborne systems $138.5M (to 2030);
  Navy ATC $65.7M; AF avionics-test-equipment $98.9M (Feb 2026, to 2037); Navy
  combat integration/ID $73.4M (May 2025).
- Test Range Instrumentation Systems product line (radar/EO, bomb scoring,
  target tracking, threat systems) = direct T&E instrumentation adjacency.
- EW installed base: ~80% US fixed-wing, 95%+ Army rotary-wing (OEM pull-through).
- Protest posture / mixed services record: NGA GARNET protest dismissed (Sep 2025,
  Palantir OTA follow-on); MDA HQ0856 protest dismissed (Oct 2022); San Diego
  Ship Repair protest denied.
