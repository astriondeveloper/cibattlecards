# Parsons Corporation — research notes (July 2026)

Collection method and provenance. All facts below come from public sources
collected via web search on 2026-07-17. Direct page fetch (WebFetch/curl) was
blocked by this session's egress policy for most hosts, including SEC.gov,
GovConWire, StockTitan, parsons.com, GlobeNewswire, Wikipedia, and the three
government data sites (usaspending.gov, sam.gov, gao.gov all returned HTTP 403
on CONNECT). Figures therefore rest on web-search result summaries and press
coverage, not on primary documents retrieved in-session. Where a number would
normally come from FPDS/USAspending obligation data or a SAM.gov registration,
that is flagged as a gap rather than guessed.
